#GitWorkshop

Projects can be hard to track and keep, but with GitHub it can make your life easier! Don't know where or how to start? Let’s “GIT it Together” with us by registering at GDSC GitHub Workshop!

GDSC UPM are pleased to announce that we will be having a workshop on GitHub by our own dedicated core team member under the Web/App Development Department. By joining this talk, you will be able to explore and learn new skills regarding GitHub!

#TheDataTalk

Wondering how to kickstart a data science career? You are definitely at the right place!

GDSC UPM are pleased to announce that we will be having a talk on Data Science in collaboration with the industry expert, Mr. Fares Hasan, who is a Lead Data Scientist at Carsome.
